Popular LEGO car themes

A quick look at the most common lines for car fans.

Speed Champions

Known for licensed models from brands like Ferrari, Porsche, and McLaren with realistic proportions and printed details.

Technic

Larger models focused on mechanical realism, often featuring suspensions, gearboxes, and working steering systems.

Creator & Icons

From family-friendly 3-in-1 builds to premium display sets, these lines offer both play value and collector appeal.

Building and collecting tips

Simple habits that make building smoother and collecting more rewarding.

Sort first, build faster

Separate small connectors, tiles, and specialty pieces into trays before starting to reduce build interruptions.

Protect display value

Keep spare stickers, manuals, and extra parts together. Display away from direct sunlight to avoid fading.

Track your collection

Maintain a simple list of set numbers, condition, and missing pieces so trading and future rebuilds are easier.
