About the project

Use this template to present a robotics class project, a lab prototype, or an open-source platform. It’s intentionally minimal: one page, strong typography, and room for videos, links, and a technical write‑up.

Mission

Make real-world autonomy reliable: tight safety loops, repeatable tests, clear metrics.

Focus areas

Perception, mapping, navigation, and robust low-level control across uneven terrain.

Open workflow

Design docs, build logs, and experiment results in one place.
