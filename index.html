About the project

OPC Project 1 is a robotics lab exploring autonomous perception, planning, and control. Browse our platforms and experiments, follow our progress, or get in touch.

Mission

Make real-world autonomy reliable: tight safety loops, repeatable tests, clear metrics.

Focus areas

Perception, mapping, navigation, and robust low-level control across uneven terrain.

Open workflow

Design docs, build logs, and experiment results in one place.
