What we do

Applied aviation engineering and operations research.

Aircraft design

Rapid prototyping of fixed-wing airframes tuned for endurance, payload capacity, and field maintainability.

Flight systems

Integrated avionics stacks for navigation, telemetry, and mission monitoring from launch through recovery.

Validation

Structured pre-flight checks, envelope expansion, and post-flight analysis to keep every sortie safe and measurable.

Aircraft highlights

Current platforms and mission-ready configurations.

Falcon-1 trainer

Stable high-wing platform for pilot training and low-risk systems testing.

  • Wingspan: 1.8 m
  • Range: 40 km nominal
  • Payload: camera + telemetry

Swift-2 surveyor

Long-endurance airframe designed for mapping and inspection missions.

  • Wingspan: 2.4 m
  • Endurance: 95 minutes
  • Payload: dual-sensor pod

Aero-3 utility

Modular test aircraft for avionics integration and mission rehearsal.

  • Wingspan: 2.1 m
  • Cruise: 72 km/h
  • Payload: swappable bay
