History

A short timeline of how logo mini figures became a collectible category.

Early era
Promotional character figures

Brands began releasing small mascot toys tied to campaigns and retail events, setting the foundation for logo-themed collectibles.

Growth era
Blind-box collecting culture

Limited assortments and chase variants brought trading and online collector communities into the hobby.

Today
Cross-brand collaborations

Designer studios and major labels now co-release branded mini figure lines with story-driven packaging and display-ready accessories.

Collecting tips

Simple habits that help new collectors avoid common mistakes.

Verify authenticity

Check packaging details, seller history, and official release notes before buying rare variants.

Track condition

Store figures away from direct sunlight and keep boxes, inserts, and accessories together to preserve value.

Set a collecting strategy

Decide whether you collect full waves, one brand line, or artist exclusives so your budget stays focused.
